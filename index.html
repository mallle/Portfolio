My journey

I wrote my first line in HTML on January 1st, 2017. After that, there was no way back for me. Autumn 2017, I then started a 2-year vocational education training to become a developer.

I had the chance to learn SQL and some Java during my time in school. At work, I was working with PHP and Javascript, and I soon got into the world of Laravel and Vue.js.

While programming in my spare time, I build a web site (for the company Mord bei Tisch) in Vue.js and rebuild it in nuxt.js.

In summer 2019, I had the chance to change employer, and since then, I have been discovering the world of Symfony.
